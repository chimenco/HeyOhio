About Us

OHIO is a small web studio servicing new Shopify store owners around the world.

Our Shopify concierge services are focused on helping Shopify store owners in their first year of business.

We understand the limitations of going from $0 to profitable with an eCommerce project. Like you, we’ve been there. It’s not all rainbows and pancakes. It’s hard work.

Some of the biggest challenges in eCommerce come after sales start to kick in. Staffing for dev, design and marketing can break the bank if not done right.

How exactly does OHIO help you?

1. Our butler plans tackle your growth and hiring problem head on. We aim to help Shopify store owners that are in their first year of business. Cost effective growth plans to get you to year 2.

2. Our marketplace provides affordable marketing packages done for you. We curate the Shopify ecosystem to bring you the best products available at insider prices.

3. We help bigger agencies off load the menial tasks that don’t fit into project scope or to support higher level staff in agencies execute on strategy. We have a white label agency plan to cover all the bases with your end clients.
