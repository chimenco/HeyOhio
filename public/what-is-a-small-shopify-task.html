What the heck is a small Shopify task?

What the heck is a small Shopify task?

Ok, I agree. This is subjective. It depends on who is measuring the task, their skill level and what they are relating the word ”small” to.

Based on our years of experience with the web and Shopify, we define small as something that will take us 1 hour or less.

In line with our goal of delivering a super streamlined service, our team decides what is small and what is not. We approve or reject tasks based on our best judgement, that’s it. As time goes, we will add to our list of approved small tasks below to give newcomers a better idea of ”small”.

If submitted tasks are decided to be ”Big” (ie. not small) – we will either break it down for you into smaller tasks that can be submitted in sequence OR we work with vetted partners that specialize in bigger Shopify jobs.

We understand that the range of small may not work for everyone, but we’ll do our best to make sure you’re never left without options if your tasks don’t fit into our service scope.

Here are real examples of small tasks we can help you with:

Small graphic tasks:

  • 1. Branded social media images (2 per hour)
  • 2. Email newsletter graphics (2 per hour)
  • 3. Logo color adjustments
  • 4. Product image fixes (3 per hour)
  • 5. Webpage element layout mockup
  • 6. Feature blog images
  • 7. Photo manipulation (2 per hour)
  • 8. Facebook ad creative
  • 9. Google ad creative
  • 8. Answer support questions/advice

Small tech tasks:

  • 1. Shopify app and theme installs/activations
  • 2. Simple Shopify app configurations
  • 3. Webpage adjustments
  • 4. Slicing of webpage sectional elements
  • 5. Webpage speed enhancements
  • 6. Troubleshooting issues
  • 7. Create how-to screencasts
  • 8. SEO Meta data entry
  • 9. Install Google Analytics code
  • 10. Install various javascript codes
  • 11. Answer support questions/advice
  • 12. Submit tickets with Shopify and 3rd party app creators
  • 13. Change pricing

What we don’t do:

  • 1. Copywrite for your designs
  • 2. Create logos
  • 3. Redesign full web pages
  • 4. Slice full page PSD’s
  • 5. Keyword research
  • 6. Create new functionality/apps

As we get more requests, we will add to this list.

If you have task ideas that you need help with and you don’t see them on this list, please email us at tasker@heyohio.com
