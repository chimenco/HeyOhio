Frequently Asked Questions

What do you mean by a small task?

The definition of a small graphic or tech task is subjective and based on our experience in the industry. In general, we measure tasks in time and our definition of a small is a task that takes us 1 hour or less. We’ve written out examples of small design and technical tasks, here.

How does it work?

After your payment is confirmed, we send you a welcome email which contains details about the agreement and a specific email address you will use to submit your tasks. Tasks are generally approved or disapproved within 1-2 hours and turn around time is up to 24 hours, guaranteed.

Do you work weekends?

Hell yeah we do. We know first hand how hard it is to get a shop up and going. Hustling on weekends is part of the formula, so we’re there for you. That said, things do slow down a little for us on Sundays between 9am-9pm. Every good butler does need a few hours a week to polish his own shoes and press his own shirts to be on his A game for the week.

In what cases do you reject tasks?

We sometimes have to reject tasks or ask for clarification. If your tasks has several steps, we will break it down for you into 1 hour tasks to submit one at a time. If we accept a task it means we know we can do it in a timely manner. If there are delays we take responsibility for getting the job done well, guaranteed.

What if I need a bigger job done? Do you do that?

We don’t. Our business is setup to serve customers with small tasks. However, we work with expert Shopify partners that offer larger scale project work. Check out our partners page here

How do you accept payments

We only accept Paypal subscription payments. Our first love.

Can I get a refund

We don’t offer a refund guarantee. But if something goes wrong, we’re all about being fair. You can cancel anytime but we are selective about refunds and will consider on a case by case basis. Because there are startup costs for us to onboard you, and we don’t charge an onboarding fee, we won’t freely refund customers who simply have buyers remorse. Please ask questions before signing up if something isn’t clear. We read alot of gentlemen’s blogs and we think this is fair.

How awesome am I?

Well, considering you’ve decided to use Shopify and you’re checking out Carson, we’d say you’re pretty awesome.

How can I show my gratitude for this amazing service

We’re glad you asked this. We love getting testimonials but we’d love even more if you gave us a share on your favorite social network. We also accept pips in the form of Maseratis, Vintage VW Vans and sporting event tickets.
